## Day 6: AI and AI Agents: Common myths and mistakes
### Key concepts
- AI and AI Agents: Common myths and mistakes refers to the misconceptions people have about Artificial Intelligence and AI Agents. A plain-English definition of AI and AI Agents is a computer system that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. Real examples include virtual assistants like Siri and Alexa, and important vocabulary includes terms like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
### Examples
```python
# Example of a simple AI agent
import random

class AI_Agent:
    def __init__(self, name):
        self.name = name
    def greet(self):
        print(f'Hello, my name is {self.name}')

agent = AI_Agent('Nila')
agent.greet()
```
