## Day 73: AI and AI Agents Deployment Thinking
### Key concepts
- Deployment thinking in AI and AI Agents involves considering the practical aspects of implementing and maintaining AI systems in real-world environments. This includes factors such as scalability, reliability, and security, as well as ensuring that the system is fair, transparent, and accountable. Advanced patterns and tradeoffs are critical in deployment thinking, as they enable the creation of sophisticated AI systems that can learn, reason, and interact with their environment in complex ways.
