### Key concepts
- Project goal refers to the objective or target that an AI or AI agent is designed to achieve. A step-by-step plan involves breaking down the project into smaller, manageable tasks to ensure successful completion. Testing the result is crucial to evaluate the performance and effectiveness of the AI or AI agent.
