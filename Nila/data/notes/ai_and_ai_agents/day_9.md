## Day 9: AI and AI Agents basic workflow
### Key concepts
- AI and AI Agents basic workflow refers to the series of steps and processes that are involved in designing, developing, and deploying AI and AI Agents. The core idea is that AI and AI Agents involve a continuous cycle of data collection, processing, and feedback, and a simple exercise is to identify and describe the different stages of the AI and AI Agents workflow. A common beginner mistake is to overlook the importance of testing and validation.
### Examples
```python
# Example of a simple AI workflow
import random

class AI_Workflow:
    def __init__(self, name):
        self.name = name
    def collect_data(self):
        print(f'Collecting data for {self.name}...')
    def process_data(self):
        print(f'Processing data for {self.name}...')
    def deploy(self):
        print(f'Deploying {self.name}...')

workflow = AI_Workflow('Nila')
workflow.collect_data()
workflow.process_data()
workflow.deploy()
```
