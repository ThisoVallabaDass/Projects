## Day 37: AI and AI Agents Building a Practical Project
### Key concepts
- Building a practical project in AI and AI Agents involves applying theoretical concepts to real-world problems. This requires a deep understanding of the project goal, a step-by-step plan, and thorough testing of the result. The project goal should be specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).
