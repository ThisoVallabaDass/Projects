## Day 17: AI and AI Agents Debugging and Review
### Key concepts
- The core idea of AI and AI Agents debugging and review is to identify and fix errors or bugs in the AI agent.
- A simple exercise in this context could be to use a debugger to step through the code and identify any issues.
- A common beginner mistake is to not fully test the AI agent before deploying it.
### Examples
```python
import pdb
pdb.set_trace()
```
### Practice notes
- Students should practice debugging and reviewing their AI agents using popular tools and techniques.
- They should also focus on testing their agents thoroughly to ensure they work as expected.
