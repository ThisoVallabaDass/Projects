## Day 36: AI and AI Agents Intermediate Project Planning
### Key concepts
- The project goal in AI and AI Agents refers to the specific objective or outcome that the project aims to achieve. This could be developing a chatbot, creating a recommender system, or building a predictive model. A step-by-step plan is essential for achieving this goal, as it breaks down the project into manageable tasks and helps to ensure that everything is completed on time. Testing the result is also crucial, as it allows developers to identify and fix any bugs or issues that may have arisen during the project.
### Examples
```python
# Example of a step-by-step plan for a chatbot project
project_goal = "Develop a chatbot that can answer customer queries"
steps = [
    "Define the scope and requirements of the project",
    "Design the chatbot's architecture and user interface",
    "Develop the chatbot's natural language processing capabilities",
    "Test and refine the chatbot"
]
```
